# 🌘 BLACK ICHOR

**The UI is your pulse. The terminal is your mind.**

Black Ichor isn't a typical menu-driven RPG. It is a high-fidelity survival terminal designed to pull you into a world of creeping rot and atmospheric dread. Built for players who want their choices to matter and their environment to feel alive, this interface transforms raw AI storytelling into a tactile, reactive simulation.

---

## 💀 A Living, Breathing Interface

### **The Pressure System**
The interface reacts to your character's mental state. As the **Atmosphere** thickens, the very screen you’re looking at begins to distort. The UI shakes, shadows creep inward from the corners, and the text pulses with a sickly light. You don't just read about fear; you see the terminal struggle to stay stable.

### **Total Narrative Recall**
Forget "Dory-brain" AI. This client features a built-in **Long-Term Memory Bank**. Every victory, wound, and whispered secret is etched into a local database. When you act, the game reaches back into your specific history, ensuring the story remembers that a door was left unlocked three chapters ago or that you’re still bleeding from a previous encounter.

### **Dynamic Vitals & Survival**
Your dashboard is a real-time readout of your survival odds. 
* **Pulse & Reserves:** Track your life force and dwindling supplies through a high-contrast HUD.
* **The Echo Meter:** Sound is a resource. Moving too fast or being too loud raises your "Echo," physically altering the UI and shifting the audio from haunting silence to aggressive, heart-pounding combat scores.
* **Vows & Sanctum:** Keep track of your objectives and your base's fortifications directly on the glass.

---

## 🎨 The Mind's Eye (Neural Visualizer)

At any moment, you can trigger the **Visualize** command. The engine takes the current scene—the smells, the lighting, the looming threats—and generates a unique, haunting image filtered through a "Deep Rot" aesthetic. It provides a visual window into the Ichor, unique to your specific journey.

## 📼 Analog Persistence
* **Physical Saves:** Your entire world—including your inventory, memories, and story log—is packaged into a single, portable file. 
* **No Accounts, No Bloat:** Just a raw, high-performance survival tool that runs directly in your browser.

## 🕯️ How to Begin
1.  **Boot the terminal** by opening the index.
2.  **Define your intent.** Talk to the interface like a narrator who is actively trying to kill you.
3.  **Watch the indicators.** If the screen starts shaking, run. 

**The Ichor remembers everything. Do you?**
